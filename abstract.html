Abstract:

Efficient Data Rollover Strategies and Management is an innovative solution to transfer unused daily or monthly data of a customer to the next day or month. Excess unused data can be sold back or distributed to new customers for lesser rates thereby avoiding wastage. With the increasing importance of staying connected in today's digital world, having a reliable data plan is crucial. Many mobile carriers offer data rollover for post-paid connections whereas prepaid users lose the data if they fail to use it.
